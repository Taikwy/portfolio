About Me
When I have free time to play video games I try to play as wide of a variety as possible. I spend a lot of time trying out new indie games to see whatever fresh mechanics or systems they might implement. I love playing challenging games like Furi and Sekiro although I'm not opposed to lighter games either. Sometimes I crave something more competitive, so I'll beat up some people in fighting games. Or maybe goof around in some first person shooters with my friends.
Whether it's digital art with my drawing tablet or pushing some pixels to make my own game assets, drawing is one of my favorite hobbies (even if I don't always have time for it). I also love cooking and testing out my own recipes to experience cuisines my local restaurants might not feature.
